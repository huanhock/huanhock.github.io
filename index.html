Work

I'm currently a quantitative investment analyst at Golden Horse Fund Management, where I spend my time looking at numbers.

I graduated with a BSc (Hons) in Quantitative Finance, and a minor in Computer Science, from the National University of Singapore.

I muse about my opinions on global politics and macro-economics from time to time here.

My Goals

My pragmatic goals are:

  • In 2 Years: Gain more comprehensive understanding of investment management by completing the Chartered Financial Analyst program
  • In 3 Years: Receive a rigorous training in Machine Learning by finishing the Georgia Tech Online Master of Science in Computer Science
  • In 5 Years: Build an investment track record by managing a portfolio for my family & myself, so that I can set up my own sustainable and socially responsible ESG fund
  • And along the way: Connect with passionate and driven people who can help me work on my moonshots

The moonshots I really hope to work on include:

  • Disrupt and level the financial playing field for the layperson
  • Change the way education works through technology, evidence-based psychology, and gamification
  • Revolutionize the state of personal computing device by moving away from the default desktop+tablet+mobile setup, and towards cloud computing
  • Develop and promote the field of AI as augmented intelligence, with the objective of complementing, rather than replacing human intelligence
Hit me up if your goals are similar to mine and you want to work on them together, or if you think my moonshots are interesting and would like to discuss them!
